Makers against Covid from France

Solidarity and cooperation to fight this pandemic!


The Covid-Initiatives platform draws an inventory of initiatives from the civil society to help fight the Covid-19 pandemic. Many citizens have started mobilizing since the start of the health crisis, assembling collective actions via multiple exchange platforms, and self-organizing to provide solutions and emergency measures. Everywhere, solidarity and cooperation enable the development of dependable solutions, to maintain access to education, supply food to those in need, and assist isolated individuals, or to design medical device prototypes to meet the urgent needs.


Initiatives are popping up all around so fast that it has become difficult to navigate the profusion of information.


Our website intends to put together a comprehensive inventory of these initiatives and feature the projects emerging from civic mobilization as intelligibly as possible. Above all, we aim to help the Makers - engineers, artisans, DYIers, creators of all backgrounds, civic contributors - who take action in a situation of emergency.
